Rancocas Valley Track Club Donation and Sponsorship Opportunities

RVTC has a 3 tier donation program:
 
            GOLD - $1000
 
            SILVER - $500
 
            BRONZE - $100
 
We also have a sponsorship program called THE RVTC MAGNIFICENT 7

This program selects 7 athletes that exemplify the conditions set forth by the program. 

A RVTC Magnificent 7 sponsor is asked to pay the registration fees for the indoor and outdoor session of the 7 athletes selected for the program. The current registration fees will dictate the sponsorship requirement. The insignia of the sponsor will be placed on either shirts or shorts, or both, and be placed on the RVTC Magnificent 7 jacket.

Please call for details. (609-267-8253).
 
Donations from $50 to $100 will be given the designation of Honorable Mention.
