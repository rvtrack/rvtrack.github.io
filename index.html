Rancocas Valley Track Club

In existence since 1999, we are a non-profit organization training athletes from the surrounding communities including Westampton, Lumberton, Eastampton, Mt. Holly, Mt. Laurel, Hainesport, Pemberton, Willingboro, Moorestown, and Delran.

Track and Field Opportunities

For Students ages 5 - 18 years old
See information shown below or download our Flyer from our Forms page.

Some of the key benefits of participation:

  • Learn the sport of track & field
  • Compete in many Saturday track meets
  • Develop strength, speed, flexibility and endurance
  • Meet new people and have fun!!

How To Register: Complete and send in the registration form. For more information contact Head Coach Keith Barr at 609-267-8253 or e-mail us at: info@rvtrack.org

** This club is not affiliated with any school district and is NOT a school function. **

Summer Intensity Camp

Intensity Camp is a camp designed for athletes from ages 8 to 18.  An athlete must be 8 years old by December 31, 2016 in order to participate.  This camp is not a recreational camp! It will be intense with a lot of nonstop running and exercise. The camp will run from 6:30 – 8:30 dates are July 12th to August 19th. All practices will be held at the Rancocas Valley track behind the high school on Wednesdays and Fridays. Registration ends June 15th for the summer program, which costs $110.00 per athlete.
