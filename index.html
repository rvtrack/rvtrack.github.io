Rancocas Valley Track Club

In existence since 1999, we are a non-profit organization. RV Track Club is a feeder program for the Rancocas Valley Regional High School.


New: Help support our track club by purchasing RV Track branded gear!

Please visit our new Rancocas Valley Track Club Fundraising Page to get RV Track branded keychains, mugs, datebooks, notebooks and clothing.

Track and Field Opportunities

For Students ages 5 - 18 years old
See information shown below or download our Flyer from our Forms page.

Some of the key benefits of participation:

  • Learn the sport of track & field
  • Compete in many Saturday track meets
  • Develop strength, speed, flexibility and endurance
  • Meet new people and have fun!!

How To Register: Register for the upcoming season here. For more information e-mail us at: info@rvtrack.org

** This club is not affiliated with any school district and is NOT a school function. **

Upcoming: Winter Season

Winter Session will begin January 3rd and end February 16th. The location will be at the Holly Hills Elementary School All Purpose Room. It will be held every Tuesday and Thursday. The 5 to 8 year olds will start at 6:45 with the doors opening at 6:40 pm. The training session will end at 7:30 pm. The older group will start at 7:40 pm and end at 8:30 pm.


We will attend at least 1 track meet for all athletes. Please bring bottled water wear comfortable running shoes. Do not come with brand new running shoes. Having new shoes will make your athlete have a difficult time running with hurt feet. Shorts and t-shirts are recommended. Please use the registration link to sign up for the winter session. Registration is now open for the winter program.

Registration Cost: $125 per athlete

The spring registration will come later in January.
