Personal Training is Now Available!

Upcoming Program: Spring Track and Field

When: July 19th through August 19th (Note: Changed from original announcement)

Time: Monday and Friday evenings 6:30-8:30 PM
Location: Rancocas Valley Regional High School
Cost: $110 per athlete, not to exceed $220 per family

You can participate in track meets, but it is not mandatory. Track meets start at 5:30 with registration. Track meets will be held at Moorestown High School on Bridgeboro Road. Dates to be determined.

You can download our Intensity Camp Flyer and Registration Form from our Forms page.
Some of the key benefits of participation:
  • Learn the sport of track & field
  • Compete in many Saturday track meets
  • Develop strength, speed, flexibility and endurance
  • Meet new people and have fun!!
  • All athletes will receive a team T-shirt

How To Register: Complete and send in the registration form. For more information contact Head Coach Keith Barr at 609-267-8253 or e-mail us at: info@rvtrack.org

** This club is not affiliated with any school district and is NOT a school function. **
