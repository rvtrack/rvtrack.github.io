Preparing Athletes of the Future

Rancocas Valley Track Club

In existence since 1999, we are a non-profit organization training athletes from the surrounding communities including Westampton, Lumberton, Eastampton, Mt. Holly, Mt. Laurel, Hainesport, Pemberton, Willingboro, Moorestown, and Delran.

Track and Field Opportunities

For Students ages 5 - 18 years old
See information shown below or download our Flyer from our Forms page.

Some of the key benefits of participation:

  • Learn the sport of track & field
  • Compete in many Saturday track meets
  • Develop strength, speed, flexibility and endurance
  • Meet new people and have fun!!

How To Register: Complete and send in the registration form. For more information contact Head Coach Keith Barr at 609-267-8253 or e-mail us at: info@rvtrack.org

** This club is not affiliated with any school district and is NOT a school function. **

Upcoming: Winter Season

Indoor practices will be held for Ages 5-8 from 6:45 – 7:30 on Tuesday and Thursday evenings at Holly Hills Elementary School in Westampton. Practices for Ages 9 -15 will follow on those same evenings from 7:40 – 8:30. The season will begin on January 8th and end on February 14th. We plan to attend two indoor track meets at Moorestown High School to compete against other area track clubs on January 30th and February 6th. The Wednesday track meets will take the place of the Thursday practice the same week. All athletes will receive a team t-shirt and are expected to wear running sneakers which TIE at all times and solid navy blue or black shorts at meets. The winter session is $85 for athletes ages 5-8 and $105 for athletes ages 9 and up. Team size is limited due to space constraints, so please register early! Registration will end January 7th

Upcoming: Spring Season

Practices for ages 5-8 will be from 6:00 – 7:00 on Monday evenings. Ages 9-18 will practice on Tuesday and Thursday evenings from 6:00 – 7:30. The season will run from March 18 - May 30th. All practices will be held on the all-weather track at Rancocas Valley Regional HS in Mt. Holly. Track meets (approximately 8) are usually held on Saturdays starting in April (except Easter and Memorial Day Weekends) at South Jersey area tracks. Some meets for the 5-8 Group may be held on Friday evenings. The meet schedule will be available in March. Spring Track season will cost $85 for 5-8 and $125 for 9 and older. All athletes are expected to wear running sneakers which TIE at all times and solid navy blue or black shorts at meets. Athletes will receive a team jersey to be worn at all track meets. A $15 cash refund will made when the jersey is returned at the end of the season. Registration for the spring season ends March 17th.
