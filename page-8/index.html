Rancocas Valley Track Club

In existence since 1999, we are a non-profit organization. RV Track Club is a feeder program for the Rancocas Valley Regional High School.


New: Help support our track club by purchasing RV Track branded gear!

Please visit our new Rancocas Valley Track Club Fundraising Page to get RV Track branded keychains, mugs, datebooks, notebooks and clothing.

Track and Field Opportunities

For Students ages 5 - 18 years old
See information shown below or download our Flyer from our Forms page.

Some of the key benefits of participation:

  • Learn the sport of track & field
  • Compete in many Saturday track meets
  • Develop strength, speed, flexibility and endurance
  • Meet new people and have fun!!

How To Register: Complete and send in the registration form. For more information contact Head Coach Keith Barr at 609-267-8253 or e-mail us at: info@rvtrack.org

** This club is not affiliated with any school district and is NOT a school function. **

Upcoming: Winter Season

Strength & Conditioning Track Practices will be held for Ages 5-8 from 7:05 – 7:45 PM on Tuesday and Thursday evenings at the Holly Hills Elementary School in Westampton. Practices for Ages 9 -15 will follow on those same evenings from 7:55 – 8:45. The season will begin on January 9th and end on February 15th. We will be attending two indoor track meets at Moorestown High School to compete against other area track athletes and track clubs. Team size will be limited due to space constraints, so register early!

Upcoming: Spring Season

Practices for ages 5-8 will be from 6:00 – 7:00 PM on Monday evenings. Ages 9-18 will practice on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 6:00 – 7:30. The season will run from March 19th - May 31st . All practices will be held on the all-weather track at Rancocas Valley HS in Mt. Holly. Track meets will be held some Saturdays at South Jersey area tracks
