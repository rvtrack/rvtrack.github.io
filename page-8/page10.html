Rancocas Valley Track Club

In existence since 1999, we are a non-profit organization. RV Track Club is a feeder program for the Rancocas Valley Regional High School.

New: Registration is open for the 2021 Spring Season, which starts the week of March 22nd! See details in the Spring section at the bottom of the page.

Help support our track club by purchasing RV Track branded gear!

Please visit our new Rancocas Valley Track Club Fundraising Page to get RV Track branded keychains, mugs, datebooks, notebooks and clothing.

Track and Field Opportunities

For Students ages 5 - 18 years old
See information shown below or download our Flyer from our Forms page.

Some of the key benefits of participation:

  • Learn the sport of track & field
  • Compete in many Saturday track meets
  • Develop strength, speed, flexibility and endurance
  • Meet new people and have fun!!

How To Register: Register online here. For more information contact Head Coach Keith Barr at: info@rvtrack.org

** This club is not affiliated with any school district and is NOT a school function. **

New: Spring Season Registration

Spring registration is now open. The spring session will start March 21st and end June 8th.

Athletes 8 and under will begin at 6 pm and end at 7 pm on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Athletes 9 and over will start at 7:15 pm and end at 8:30 pm.

The location of the practices will be at the Rancocas Valley Regional High School behind the school at 520 Jacksonville Road, Mt. Holly.

Please bring water, wear comfortable shoes, gym pants/shorts, T-shirt/sweats. Make sure your athlete eats something no later than an hour before practice. For example if practice starts at 6 pm eat before 5 pm.

Registration cost is $125 for all athletes. Please use the registration link.

We look forward to seeing everyone. Looking forward to another great season!
