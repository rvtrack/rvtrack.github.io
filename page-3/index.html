Personal Training Opportunities with Keith Barr

Hello. My name is Keith Barr. I bring the best in establishing routines for losing weight, getting toned, preparing for competition and improving your quality of life. My experience and expertise breads success and confidence. I bring flexibility and strength to the table. You will get immediate and continued results. My clients have lost weight, gained muscle and strength, passed PT tests for the Army, excelled in sports in particular track and 5Ks, as well as rehabilitating clients with bad backs. It is important to me to be punctual. My passion is to see my clients succeed. I am cognizant that people have lives too and that I will work with you to figure out a training plan for you. I am compassionate and understand people have certain abilities. I have a certification in personal training from WITS. My background includes 20 years in physical therapy offices as well as athletics. I have been teaching kids up to 18 years of age the fine art of track. Having a background in therapy and athletics gives me a good blend of experience which would give my client the best chance at being successful at their goals. I have certifications in CPR and First Aid. Also, my degree is a Bachelor of Science in Biomedical Engineering which entails function of the human body systems including musculoskeletal system. What you get with me is a person who will do the work of planning your workouts, establishing goals at each session, making adjustments as you progress and letting you know when to push and when to back off. You will not have to think about what you need to do next or struggle with trying to figure out what would work best. I would do all of that for you! My background in healthcare would help you with decisions about your own healthcare. Nutrition would not be left out. Please contact me to get the best training that you are ever going to get! You can look me up on LinkedIn.
